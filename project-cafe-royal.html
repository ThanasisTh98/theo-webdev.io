Cafe Royal Website

A comprehensive UI/UX design project for Cafe Royal, focusing on creating an elegant, user-friendly digital experience that captures the essence of premium coffee culture.

#design-overview

Design Challenge

Cafe Royal needed a digital presence that would reflect their premium coffee experience while being accessible to both coffee enthusiasts and casual visitors. The challenge was to balance sophistication with usability.

Design Solution

I created a comprehensive design system in Figma that emphasizes warm, inviting colors, elegant typography, and intuitive navigation. The design focuses on showcasing their coffee culture while making online ordering seamless.

Design Goals Achieved

  • Modern, responsive design system
  • Intuitive user experience flow
  • Brand-consistent visual identity
  • Accessible design principles
  • Mobile-first approach

Project Details

Duration: 3 months

Type: UI/UX Design

My Role: Lead Designer

Status: Design Phase

Platform: Figma

#design-process

Research

User research, competitor analysis, and market trends study to understand the coffee shop industry.

Wireframing

Created low-fidelity wireframes to establish the site structure and user flow.

Visual Design

Developed the visual identity, color schemes, typography, and component library in Figma.

Prototyping

Created interactive prototypes to demonstrate user interactions and transitions.

#key-features

Designed Features

  • Menu browsing interface

  • Store locator design
  • About us storytelling page
  • Contact and reservation forms
  • Loyalty program interface
  • Mobile app design concepts
  • Social media integration
  • Accessibility considerations
